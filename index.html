Coaching philosophy

"Most golfers don't need to be told what's wrong. They need someone who can show them how to fix it — and make sure it actually sticks."

Every lesson I give starts by listening. Where you are, what's frustrating you, what you've tried. From there, we build a focused plan around your game — not a generic curriculum. Whether you're a complete beginner or an experienced player chasing a lower handicap, the approach is the same: targeted, patient, and built around you.

Beginner Lessons

About Tom

Tom Reid —
PGA Coach & Club Fitter

I'm a PGA qualified golf coach based in Cambridgeshire. By day I work professionally as a golf fitter — which means I understand the technical relationship between equipment and mechanics at a level most coaches don't.

That knowledge directly benefits my coaching. When I watch a golfer, I'm not just seeing the swing — I'm seeing everything that influences it. Most of my coaching clients come through recommendation, often from players I've fitted or their families who've seen the change first-hand.

My lessons are relaxed, focused, and built entirely around the individual. I'll meet you where you are, work at a pace that suits you, and give you the kind of feedback that actually makes a difference out on the course.

If you're considering lessons, I'm always happy to have an initial conversation. There's no pressure and no commitment until you've decided it's right for you.
